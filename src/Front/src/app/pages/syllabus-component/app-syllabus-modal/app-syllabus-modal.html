2. GOALS, OBJECTIVES AND LEARNING OUTCOMES OF THE COURSE

The Course Goal is to form a social and humanitarian worldview (outlook) of students in the context of solving the problems of modernization public consciousness, determined by the State Program “View into future: modernization of public consciousness”.

The objectives of the course are:
  1. mastering the basic social, political and humanitarian concepts, theories and approaches...
  2. formation of ideas about the basic principles of functioning modern society...
  3. development of skills for describing and analyzing actual problems of modern society...
  4. mastering by students of the main sources and methods of obtaining information...
  5. instilling the skills to use the knowledge gained in professional activity;
  6. the formation of critical thinking skills and the ability applying it in practice.
Learning outcomes of the course:

Students successfully completing the course will be able to:

  1. explain and interpret subject knowledge (concepts, ideas, theories)...
  2. explain the socio-ethical values of society as a product integration processes...
  3. algorithmically represent the use of scientific methods and research techniques...
  4. explain the nature of situations in various spheres of social communication...
  5. provide reasoned and reasonable information about various stages of development of Kazakh society...
  6. analyze the features of social, political, cultural, psychological institutions...
  7. correctly express and reasonably defend their own opinion on issues of social importance.
